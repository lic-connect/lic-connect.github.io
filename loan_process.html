LIC Loan

(FORM NO 5196)

Eligibility

Ensure your policy qualifies for a loan. Most traditional endowment, money-back, and whole-life policies are eligible. The loan amount is typically up to 85-90% of the policy's surrender value.

Prepare Necessary Documents

  • Original Policy Document: This is the primary collateral.
  • Loan Application Form (No. 5196).
  • ID & Address Proof: Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, etc.
  • Cancelled Cheque: For direct NEFT transfer of the loan amount.

Fill Out the Loan Application Form

Fill in your personal details, policy number, and the desired loan amount. Provide your bank details for the transfer and attach all necessary documents. Sign the form where required.

Visit the Nearest LIC Branch

Submit the filled application form and documents to your servicing branch. An official will verify the documents and provide an acknowledgment receipt.

Loan Agreement and Terms

Once approved, you may need to sign a loan agreement. The interest rate, usually lower than personal loans, will be communicated by LIC. Interest must be paid regularly to prevent it from being added to the principal loan amount.

Receiving the Loan Amount

The approved loan amount will be credited directly to your bank account via NEFT or issued as a cheque.
