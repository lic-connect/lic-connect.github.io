A Lifetime of Benefits

Guaranteed Annual Income

After your premium paying term ends, you receive a guaranteed income for life. This includes:

  • An annual payment of 8% of the Basic Sum Assured.
  • This survival benefit continues every year until age 100 or until death, whichever is earlier.

On Death or Maturity

The plan provides a substantial lump sum for your family's future, whenever it's needed.

  • On Death: The nominee receives the Sum Assured on Death + Vested Bonuses + FAB.
  • On Maturity (at age 100): The policyholder receives the Basic Sum Assured + Vested Bonuses + FAB.

The Umang Advantage: Income + Protection

This plan offers the best of both worlds: a regular, tax-free pension-like income in your later years, and a robust, ever-growing life cover that protects your family throughout your entire life.

Loan and Surrender Value are available after 2 full years' premium payment. For details, contact us.
