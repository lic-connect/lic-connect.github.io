About LIC-Connect

LIC-Connect is a dynamic web platform designed to streamline and enhance the experience of life insurance policyholders, agents, and prospects. Built with modern web technologies and integrated with Supabase, this application serves as a one-stop solution for tracking, managing, and understanding life insurance-related services with a focus on the Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC).

Our Aim

Our aim is to provide users with a reliable interface to access premium calculators, policy benefits, maturity and surrender value estimators, and other useful tools. LIC-Connect empowers users to make informed financial decisions by offering clear, real-time insights based on their policy data. The platform is especially useful for LIC agents to manage their leads and clients effectively.

Our Beliefs

We believe in transparency, ease of use, and data security. LIC-Connect is not affiliated with LIC but is independently created to assist users in understanding and managing their LIC policies better.
