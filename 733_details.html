Unmatched Benefits

On Maturity

On successful completion of the policy term, you receive a substantial payout to fulfill your goals, consisting of:

  • The full Basic Sum Assured.
  • All accumulated Simple Reversionary Bonuses.
  • A Final Additional Bonus, if declared.

On Survival

On survival to the end of the policy term, a lump sum amount equal to Basic Sum Assured along with vested Simple Reversionary Bonuses and Final Additional Bonus, if any, shall be payable.

The Lakshya Benefit: Uninterrupted Support

In case of death, the plan ensures your family's goals are not disturbed. The nominee receives an Annual Income Benefit of 10% of the Sum Assured every year until the maturity date, AND a final lump sum of 110% of the Sum Assured + Bonuses on the maturity date.

Loan and Surrender Value are available after 2 full years' premium payment. For details, contact us.
