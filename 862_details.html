Saral Pension

Plan 862

A Simple Pension for a Worry-Free Life.

A standardized, non-participating, single premium immediate annuity plan that offers a fixed, regular income for life with a return of your initial investment to your family.

Premium ModeSingle Payment Only
Annuity TypeImmediate
Entry Age40 to 80 Years
Annuity OptionsSingle or Joint Life

Benefit Structure

For Life: You receive a fixed pension at regular intervals (monthly, quarterly, etc.) for your entire life.

On Death: After your lifetime, 100% of the purchase price (your initial investment) is returned to your nominee.

Loan facility is available after 6 months from the date of commencement.
