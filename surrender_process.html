Policy Surrender

(Form No. 5074)

Gather Documents

You will need the Original Policy Bond, Surrender Form 5074, ID Proof, and a cancelled cheque for bank details.

Fill Up Form 5074

Enter your personal details, policy number, and bank account information. Sign the form, getting it notarized or witnessed if required.

Submit at Branch

Visit your servicing LIC branch, submit all the documents, and ensure you get an acknowledgment receipt for your records.

Verification & Payout

LIC will verify the documents and process the claim. The surrender value is typically credited to your account in 7-10 working days.
