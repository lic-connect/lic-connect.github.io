Policy Benefits

On Survival (Maturity)

At the end of the policy term, you receive a handsome lump sum consisting of:

  • Basic Sum Assured
  • Vested Simple Reversionary Bonuses
  • Final Additional Bonus (if any)

On Death

In case of an unfortunate event, your nominee will receive the higher of:

  • Sum Assured on Death + Bonuses
  • 7 times of Annualized Premium
  • 105% of all Premiums Paid

Additional Advantages

Loan Facility

Available after payment of premiums for 2 full years.

Tax Benefits

Premiums paid are eligible for tax rebate under Sec 80C. Maturity is tax-free under Sec 10(10D).

Surrender Value

The policy acquires a surrender value after 2 full years' premiums have been paid.

Proposal Form 300/340/360 shall be used for this plan.
