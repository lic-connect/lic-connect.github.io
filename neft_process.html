NEFT Mandate Form

(FORM NO 5202)

1. Obtain the Form

Download Form No. 5202 from the official LIC website or collect a physical copy from any LIC branch office.

2. Fill Personal & Bank Details

  • **Personal Info:** Fill in your full name, policy number(s), email, and mobile number.
  • **Bank Info:** Provide the account holder's name, account number, account type (Savings/Current), bank name, branch address, and the 11-character IFSC code.

3. Attach Necessary Documents

Attach a cancelled cheque with your name pre-printed on it. If unavailable, a photocopy of the first page of your bank passbook showing all details is acceptable.

4. Declaration and Signature

Sign the form in the designated space. Ensure the signature matches the one on LIC's records. Mention the date and place.

5. Submit the Form

Submit the filled and signed form with documents to your nearest LIC branch. Request an acknowledgment receipt as proof of submission.
