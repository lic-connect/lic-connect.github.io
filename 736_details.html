Comprehensive Benefits

On Maturity

On surviving the full policy term, you receive a lump sum payout to fulfill your financial goals, which includes:

  • The full Basic Sum Assured.
  • All accumulated Simple Reversionary Bonuses.
  • A Final Additional Bonus, if declared.

On Death

In case of an unfortunate event, the death benefit paid to the nominee is the highest of:

  • Basic Sum Assured + Bonuses.
  • 7 times the annual premium + Bonuses.
  • 105% of all premiums paid to date.

Loan and Surrender Value are available after 2 full years' premium payment. For details, contact us.
