Survival Benefit

(Form No. 5180)

Step-by-Step Guide

Check Notifications

Check your policy schedule for due dates. LIC typically sends notifications 2-3 months before a survival benefit is due.

Prepare Documents

  • Original Policy Bond
  • Survival Benefit Claim Form (No. 5180)
  • ID Proof (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.)
  • Cancelled Cheque or Bank Passbook copy
  • Self-attested Photographs (if required)

Fill the Claim Form

Fill in your personal details, policy number, and bank account information. Attach the necessary documents and sign the form.

Submit at Branch

Visit your servicing LIC branch, submit the documents, and obtain an acknowledgment receipt.

Receive Benefit

After processing (usually 7-10 working days), the survival benefit amount is credited directly to your bank account via NEFT.
