Self-Certification Form (FATCA/CRS)

(Relating to Section 285BA of the Income-tax Act, 1961)

Mandatory Information

All information must be provided by the Policyholder, Annuitant, Beneficiary, or other relevant parties.

  • Customer ID / Policy Number
  • Name and Role (e.g., Policy Holder)
  • Residence for Tax Purposes outside India: YES / NO

Declaration for Non-Residence

If you are NOT a tax resident outside India, you must certify this, sign, and date the form. You undertake to inform LIC of any changes immediately.

Declaration for Foreign Tax Residency

If you ARE a tax resident in a jurisdiction outside India, you must mandatorily provide the following details:

Country of Tax ResidencyAddressTax ID Number (TIN)
   

Declaration & Undertakings

By signing, you certify that all information is true and correct, and you agree to comply with all relevant laws for reporting. You authorize LIC to collect, store, and process this information as required for legal compliance.
