The Bachat Benefit: Loyalty Addition

On maturity, or on death after 5 policy years, you receive Loyalty Additions as a reward for staying invested.

Benefit Structure

On Maturity

On surviving the policy term, you receive the Basic Sum Assured along with any accrued Loyalty Additions.

On Death

In case of an unfortunate event, the nominee receives the "Sum Assured on Death", which is the highest of the Basic Sum Assured, 7 times the annual premium, or 105% of all premiums paid.

Loan facility available after 3 full years' premium payment. Surrender available after 1 year.
