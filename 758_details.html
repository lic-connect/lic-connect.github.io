Plan 758

A Guaranteed Pension for Life.

This is a single premium, deferred annuity plan that provides you with a guaranteed income stream after a chosen deferment period, ensuring a financially independent retired life.

Premium ModeSingle Payment Only
Annuity TypeDeferred
Deferment Period1 to 12 Years
Annuity ModesYearly, Halfly, etc.

How it Works

Pay a lump sum today and choose when you want your pension to start (1 to 12 years later). Your annuity rates are locked in at the time of purchase, providing a guaranteed income for life.

Loan and Surrender Value are available under specific options. For more details, contact your advisor.
