Maturity Claim

(Form No. 3825)

Check Maturity Date

Refer to your policy bond to confirm the date. LIC usually sends a pre-maturity intimation letter 2-3 months in advance.

Gather Documents

  • Original Policy Bond
  • Discharge Form (Form No. 3825)
  • ID Proof (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.)
  • Cancelled Cheque or Bank Passbook Copy

Fill the Discharge Form

Enter your personal details, policy number, and bank account details for NEFT transfer. Sign the form, and get it witnessed if required.

Submit Documents

Visit your servicing LIC branch in person or send the documents by registered post. Always get an acknowledgment receipt.

Processing & Verification

LIC will verify the documents. The process typically takes 7-10 working days for a straightforward claim. You will be notified upon approval.

Receive Funds

The maturity amount, including sum assured and any accrued bonuses, will be credited directly to your bank account via NEFT.
