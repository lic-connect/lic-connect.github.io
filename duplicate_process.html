Duplicate Policy Form

(Form No. 3756 & 3815)

1

Report the Loss

It is advisable to file a First Information Report (FIR) at the nearest police station to report the loss of the policy bond.

2

Prepare Documents

  • Indemnity Bond (Form 3756): A legal document indemnifying LIC against future claims on the lost bond. Must be on non-judicial stamp paper.
  • Declaration of Loss (Form 3815): A form declaring the circumstances of the loss.
  • ID & Address Proof: Your Aadhaar, PAN, etc.
  • Original Premium Receipts: If available.
3

Submit at Branch

Visit your servicing LIC branch and submit all the required documents. Ensure you get an acknowledgment receipt.

4

Processing & Issuance

LIC will verify the documents. In some cases, a newspaper advertisement may be required. The process may take a few weeks, after which a duplicate policy bond will be issued.
