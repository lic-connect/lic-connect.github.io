Policy Revival Process

A. Revival Application Form (No. 680)

For regular lapsed policies.

Personal & Policy Details

  • Policy Number, Name, DOB, Address
  • Date of Lapse, Sum Assured

Revival & Health Details

  • Revival Mode (Regular, Limited, etc.)
  • Health and Lifestyle Declaration

B. Single Premium Revival (Form No. 700)

For reviving single premium policies.

Personal & Policy Details

  • Policy Number, Name, DOB, Address
  • Date of Lapse, Sum Assured

Revival Details

  • Specify revival is for a single premium policy.
  • Provide payment and health details.

C. Limited Premium Revival (Form No. 720)

For reviving limited premium payment policies.

Personal & Policy Details

  • Policy Number, Name, DOB, Address
  • Date of Lapse, Sum Assured, Premium Details

Revival & Health Details

  • Specify revival is for a limited premium policy.
  • Describe payment plan and disclose health changes.
