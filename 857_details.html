Plan 857

Immediate Pension, Lifelong Security.

An immediate annuity plan where you pay a lump sum once and receive a regular, guaranteed pension for life. This plan offers a wide range of annuity options to suit your specific needs.

Premium ModeSingle Payment Only
Annuity TypeImmediate
Entry Age25 to 85 Years
Annuity Options10 Options Available

Benefit Structure

For Life: You receive a fixed pension at your chosen interval (monthly, quarterly, etc.) starting immediately after purchase.

On Death: The death benefit depends on the option chosen. It can range from no payout to a full return of the purchase price to your nominee.

No loan or surrender value is typically available under this plan. Proposal Form 440 (IA) is used.
