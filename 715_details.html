A Plan with Dual Benefits

On Maturity

Upon surviving the policy term, you receive a lump sum payout to fulfill your financial goals. This includes:

  • The full Basic Sum Assured.
  • All accumulated Simple Reversionary Bonuses.
  • A Final Additional Bonus, if declared.

On Death

In case of death during the policy term, a comprehensive death benefit is paid to the nominee, which is the highest of:

  • 125% of the Basic Sum Assured + Bonuses.
  • 7 times the annual premium + Bonuses.
  • 105% of all premiums paid to date.

The Anand Benefit: Lifelong Protection

Even after you receive the full maturity amount, the risk cover on your life continues. An additional Basic Sum Assured is payable to your nominee upon your death at any time after the policy term.

Loan and Surrender Value are available after 2 full years' premium payment. Proposal Form 300. For more details, contact us.
