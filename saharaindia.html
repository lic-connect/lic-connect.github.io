Endowment Plans

Sahara Dhan Sanchay Jeevan Bima

Sum Assured: Min: ₹50,000
Age at Entry: Min: 18 yrs; Max: 55 yrs
Policy Term: Min: 10 yrs; Max: 25 yrs
Premium Term: Regular Pay

Maturity Benefit

Basic Sum Assured + Vested Simple Reversionary Bonuses + Final Additional Bonus, if any.

Death Benefit

Sum Assured + Vested Bonuses. The death benefit would not be less than 105% of the total premiums paid.

Money Back Plans

Sahara Pay Back Jeevan Bima

Sum Assured: Min: ₹75,000
Age at Entry: Min: 13 yrs; Max: 50 yrs
Policy Term: 15, 20, or 25 years
Premium Term: Limited Pay

Survival Benefit

Yes. A pre-defined percentage of the Sum Assured was paid at regular intervals during the policy term. For a 20-year term, payouts were typically made at the end of the 5th, 10th, and 15th years.

Maturity Benefit

The remaining portion of the Sum Assured + Vested Simple Reversionary Bonuses + Final Additional Bonus, if any.

Death Benefit

The full Sum Assured + accrued bonuses were paid, irrespective of any survival benefits already disbursed.

Sahara Dhanvarsha Jeevan Bima

Sum Assured: Min: ₹50,000
Age at Entry: Min: 18 yrs; Max: 55 yrs
Policy Term: 12, 15, or 18 years
Premium Term: Limited Pay

Survival Benefit

Yes. Periodic survival benefits were paid out during the policy term.

Maturity Benefit

Final survival benefit installment + any guaranteed additions.

Death Benefit

The full Sum Assured was paid to the nominee.

Children's Plans

Sahara Ankur Child Plan

Sum Assured: Min: ₹50,000
Age at Entry: Parent: 20-50; Child: 0-10
Policy Term: Matures at child's age 18 or 21
Premium Term: Regular Pay

Maturity Benefit

A lump sum amount, including the Sum Assured and vested bonuses, was paid at the policy's maturity.

Death Benefit (on parent's death)

The plan featured an in-built waiver of premium benefit. Upon the policyholder's death, future premiums were waived, but the policy continued, and all planned benefits were paid to the child as scheduled.

Term Insurance Plans

Sahara Kavach

Sum Assured: Min: ₹1,00,000
Age at Entry: Min: 18 yrs; Max: 55 yrs
Policy Term: Min: 5 yrs; Max: 20 yrs
Premium Term: Regular Pay

Maturity Benefit

Not Applicable. This was a pure protection plan with no maturity value.

Death Benefit

The Sum Assured was paid to the nominee as a lump sum upon the death of the life insured during the policy term.
